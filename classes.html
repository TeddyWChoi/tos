Highlander

Since their part in King Zachariel's war, Highlanders have been acting as Royal Guards for the aristocrats, protecting the palace and major cities of the Kingdom. To this day they act as the Special Forces of the Royal Military and have suffered a huge loss in the Fallen City during Medzio Diena.
Highlanders are fearsome warriors famous for their swordsmanship that pushes enemies into each other and other objects on the field of battle.

SKILLS

  • CrosscutSlash

    Charge towards an enemy and cut a cross on their body, inflicting [Bleeding].

  • Cross GuardPierce

    Hold a defensive stance that can block with a one or two-handed sword. You can not evade while holding the stance and block chance increases. When blocking the enemy's attack, [Detect Weakness] buff is applied for 5 seconds, and the Cooldown of Cross Guard is applied for #{CaptionTime}# seconds.
    During the [Detect Weakness] buff, final damage increases when the enemy attacks.

  • DefianceNone

    Constantly applied after learning the skill
    Increases damage when attacking the boss.

  • MoulinetSlash

    Inflict damage on enemies by swinging your sword around to create continuous attacks.

  • SkylinerSlash

    Slash the enemy sideways for 2.5 seconds per 0.5 seconds.

  • Wagon WheelSlash

    Perform an upward swing with your weapon, launching an enemy up.

ATTRIBUTES

  • Wagon Wheel: Enhance

    * Increases the skill factor of [Wagon Wheel] by 0.5% per attribute level
    * +10% added increase at maximum level

    * Increases the skill factor of [Moulinet] by 0.5% per attribute level
    * +10% added increase at maximum level

    * Increases [Cross Guard]'s block increasing effects by 0.5% per attribute level
    * 10% increases additionally at maximum level

  • Skyliner: Enhance

    * +10% added increase at maximum level

    * +10% added increase at maximum level

  • Crosscut: Bleeding Duration

    * Increases the duration of [Crosscut]'s Bleeding by 1 second per attribute level

  • [Arts] Wagon Wheel: Enhanced Upgrade

    * Increases the Skill Factor of [Wagon Wheel] by 1.25% per attribute level

  • [Arts] Moulinet: Enhanced Upgrade

  • [Arts] Skyliner: Enhanced Upgrade

    * Increases the Skill Factor of [Skyliner] by 1.25% per attribute level

  • [Arts] Crosscut: Enhanced Upgrade

    * Targets: AoE Attack Ratio + 4
    * Increases range by 3 times
    * Decreases skill damage by 25%

    * Increases [Defiance]'s damage increasing effects against boss monsters by 0.5% per attribute level
    * +10% added increase at maximum level

    * (Value is applied both to the first and second slot)

CLASSES LIST

  • Swordsman Tree

    Fighter class of TOS. Basically uses melee weapons. The Swordsman is a well-balanced between DPS/Tank class with relatively high physical damage and HP.
    SEE MORE

  • Wizard Tree

    In TOS, wizards can be fostered into three types: instant casting, channeling, and spell casting, depending on the class combination. In addition, it is possible to play as a summoner.
    SEE MORE

  • Archer Tree

    The archer class is specialized in both distance and mobility. Contrary to their name, archers use a variety of ranged weapons, including poison and explosives.
    SEE MORE

  • Cleric Tree

    The cleric is the only class that can heal. They also provide useful buffs to allies and interrupt foes with debuffs. As their fundamental class cleric has a heal spell, their main role might be considered a healer, but depending on the build, they could be aggressive DPS as well.
    SEE MORE

  • Scout Tree

    The scout class mainly uses a dagger or a pistol. Scouts relied their protection on evasion and focus on offense. Their actions are agile, but need to keep an eye on the SP drain.
