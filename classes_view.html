Highlander

Since their part in King Zachariel's war, Highlanders have been acting as Royal Guards for the aristocrats, protecting the palace and major cities of the Kingdom. To this day they act as the Special Forces of the Royal Military and have suffered a huge loss in the Fallen City during Medzio Diena.
Highlanders are fearsome warriors famous for their swordsmanship that pushes enemies into each other and other objects on the field of battle.

    During the [Detect Weakness] buff, final damage increases when the enemy attacks.

Peltasta

Throughout the history of the Kingdom, the Peltasta has acted as the King's personal soldiers. To this day, they answer first and foremost to the King's orders while working as mercenaries who follow a special set of rules that are different from others.
The Peltasta are defensive warriors that specialize in shields to block the enemy's attack or taunt the enemy to lure them to their certain doom.

SKILLS

  • GuardianNone

    Reduces the incoming Physical and Magic Damage and increases the AoE Defense Ratio of the caster.
    Max. level that can be reached using items and skill effects is 7.

  • Hard ShieldNone

    Defense increases proportionally to the Defense rate of the Shield you currently equip. (Defense of the Shield with highest Defense among first slot or second slot will be applied)
    Buff will be removed when there is no Shield when changing the weapon.

  • High GuardNone

    Switch into an active defense stance using the shield. During [High Guard] buff, Evasion is unavailable but Block and Critical Resistance increases.
    Effect is removed when removing the shield equipped or re-using the skill while the buff is active.

  • LangortStrike

    Use both Shield and Sword to pierce the enemy at once. Physical Defense of the Shield is added to the Physical Attack, blocks the enemy's melee attack while attacking, and applies Silence debuff to the enemy.

  • Rim BlowStrike

    Use your shield and edge to attack and push away the enemy. If the attack is accurate, shield's physical defense is added to the physical Attack proportionally.

  • Shield LobStrike

    Throw shield to attack enemies within the front range.

  • Swash BucklingNone

    Hit your shield to draw attention from nearby enemies. The Evasion Rate and Block Rate of the threatened enemy(character, monster) decrease when hit by the caster's attack during debuff duration. Damage received by the caster's Peltasta skill increases by 10%. Additional threat rate is applied to the monster when the caster attacks and the Movement Speed of the threatened monster become faster.
    Becomes immune to Threat for a certain time after the Threat.
